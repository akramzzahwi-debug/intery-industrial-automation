Seamless Electrical Installation for Conveyor & Intralogistics Systems

INTERY D.O.O. provides specialized electrical installation services for conveyor systems and warehouse automation. Our experienced professionals ensure that your material handling systems function seamlessly, enhancing both efficiency and productivity across your entire facility.

We cover all aspects of the installation, from cable tray installations to precise wiring connections, ensuring compliance with industry standards throughout. Our clear labeling and identification systems facilitate streamlined troubleshooting and maintenance, reducing downtime and operational costs over the system's lifetime.

With expertise in various conveyor types — including high warehouses and automated sorting conveyors — we customize solutions to fit your specific operational requirements. Our team guarantees reliable power supply integration for uninterrupted operations, whether you're running a new installation or upgrading an existing system.

Key Capabilities

  • Cable tray design and installation
  • Precise motor and drive wiring
  • Power distribution for conveyor lines
  • Automated sorting conveyor integration
  • High-bay warehouse electrical systems
  • Clear labeling and identification systems
  • VDE measurements and compliance checks
