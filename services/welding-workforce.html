Certified Welding Teams for Industrial Projects Across Europe

At INTERY d.o.o., we provide highly skilled and reliable welders for a wide range of industrial projects. Our workers are fully employed by INTERY d.o.o. — not subcontracted — and are available for short-term, long-term, or project-based assignments across Europe and beyond. This direct employment model means you deal with one responsible partner and we maintain full accountability for our team's performance and conduct.

Our welding teams consist of certified professionals experienced in MIG/MAG welding, TIG welding, ARC (SMAW) welding, structural steel welding, pipe welding, and industrial assembly and maintenance. They can read and interpret technical drawings, perform on-site troubleshooting, and conduct quality assurance checks. All welders undergo strict internal testing and meet international welding standards.

They are accustomed to working in demanding industrial environments such as automotive, manufacturing, energy, and construction sectors. We offer competitive and transparent pricing, the flexibility to scale the workforce up or down based on project needs, and if required, our workers come equipped with their own PPE and tools. With fast mobilization and reliable performance, our multilingual supervisors (EN/DE) ensure effective communication throughout your project. We can also provide assistant welders, fitters, locksmiths, and complete installation teams as needed.

Welding Capabilities

  • MIG/MAG welding (GMAW)
  • TIG welding (GTAW)
  • ARC / SMAW welding
  • Structural steel welding
  • Pipe welding and fitting
  • Technical drawing interpretation
  • Quality assurance and inspection

Additional Personnel

  • Assistant welders
  • Fitters and locksmiths
  • Complete installation teams
  • EN/DE multilingual supervisors
