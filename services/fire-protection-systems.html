Protecting Your Facility with Expert Fire Protection Installation

Ensuring the safety and security of your facilities is paramount, and INTERY D.O.O. is dedicated to providing top-tier electrical installation services for fire protection systems. Our skilled team is certified and experienced in handling the most sophisticated fire protection technology, delivering reliable solutions that safeguard your assets and personnel.

We provide comprehensive services including electrical installation of fire detection and alarm systems, cable tray and cover installation along designated fire-rated routes, full component integration including detectors, call points, control panels, sounders, and strobes. All installations are accompanied by detailed labeling and documentation, along with thorough E/A checks and VDE measurements.

Our safety-first approach means every installation is verified against the applicable fire safety standards before handover. We are committed to delivering compliance, reliability, and complete peace of mind — ensuring your fire protection system will perform when it matters most.

  • Fire-rated cable tray and routing
  • Detector, call point, and sounder integration

What We Deliver

Complete Fire Protection
Installation Services

Detection & Alarm Systems

Professional installation of smoke detectors, heat detectors, flame detectors, and manual call points with full integration into the central fire alarm control panel.

Fire-Rated Cable Routes

Installation of fire-rated cable trays and conduit systems, ensuring that critical fire protection circuits maintain integrity even during a fire event as required by regulations.

Control Panel Installation

Complete wiring and commissioning of fire alarm control panels, including zone mapping, cause-and-effect programming, and integration with building management systems.

Safety Standards Compliance

All fire protection installations fully comply with applicable EN, VDE, and local fire safety standards. Certification documentation is provided for regulatory approval purposes.

Labeling & Documentation

Comprehensive as-built documentation, zone schedules, cause-and-effect matrices, and clearly labeled wiring to support ongoing maintenance, inspections, and future modifications.

E/A Checks & VDE Measurements

End-to-end acceptance testing including insulation resistance, loop resistance, and functional E/A testing of every detector zone and device before the system is handed over.
