Custom Control Cabinet Wiring for Industrial Systems

At INTERY D.O.O., we offer high-quality control cabinet wiring solutions, ensuring your industrial systems operate efficiently and reliably. Our certified electricians specialize in tailor-made wiring for control cabinets, expertly assembling and connecting essential components such as relays, PLCs, terminal blocks, and frequency converters.

We prioritize precision and clarity in every installation, providing detailed labeling and comprehensive documentation for straightforward maintenance and fault diagnosis. Every control cabinet we wire undergoes rigorous testing to verify safety and full compliance with industry standards before handover.

With capabilities for both on-site and off-site services, we adapt flexibly to your project's needs — whether you require cabinet pre-assembly in our workshop or complete on-location installation. Our experience spans a wide range of industries and cabinet types, consistently delivering exceptional quality and long-term reliability.

Key Capabilities

  • Tailor-made cabinet wiring and assembly
  • Relay, PLC, and HMI integration
  • Terminal block and bus-bar installation
  • Frequency converter wiring
  • Detailed labeling and documentation
  • Rigorous safety and compliance testing
  • On-site and off-site service options
