Reliable Electrical Installation for Power Generation Facilities

At INTERY D.O.O., we offer specialized electrical installation services for power plants, delivering reliable and efficient solutions to meet the energy demands of the modern world. With extensive experience in handling complex power generation projects, our team is equipped to provide comprehensive services tailored to each client's needs.

We handle everything from primary power distribution systems to auxiliary electrical setups, ensuring optimal performance, safety, and reliability across all electrical systems. Our offerings include cable tray installation, high-voltage power systems, control panel and cabinet installation, and thorough testing for compliance with industry standards.

Our certified technicians prioritize safety and quality in every aspect of the project, providing end-to-end support from initial consultation through to final commissioning. Partner with us for trusted, high-quality electrical installation services that ensure your power plant operates at maximum efficiency, safely and reliably.

What We Deliver

Power Plant Electrical
Installation Expertise

High-Voltage Systems

Safe, compliant installation of high-voltage power systems including switchgear, transformers, bus-bar systems, and protective relay coordination for power generation facilities.

Control Panel Installation

Complete design, wiring, and commissioning of control panels and distribution cabinets, including SCADA integration and remote monitoring system connections.

Cable Tray Systems

Engineered cable tray installation optimized for power plant environments, including fire-rated routes, segregation of power and control cables, and support for future expansion.

Auxiliary Electrical Systems

Installation of auxiliary systems including lighting, earthing, small power, battery-backed UPS systems, and all secondary electrical infrastructure required for plant operations.

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive pre-commissioning tests, VDE measurements, insulation testing, and functional verification of all electrical systems prior to plant start-up or grid connection.

Safety & Reliability

All power plant installations are delivered with the highest safety standards, full documentation packages, and compliance verification to ensure long-term operational reliability.
