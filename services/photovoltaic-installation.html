Comprehensive Solar PV Installation for Industrial Scale

At INTERY d.o.o., we are committed to supporting sustainable energy solutions with our expert photovoltaic (PV) installation services. With over a decade of industry experience, we provide comprehensive and reliable solar panel installation services designed to maximize energy efficiency and effectively harness renewable energy sources for your facility.

Our services include thorough site assessments to determine the optimal system configuration and yield, full system installation encompassing panel mounting, structural supports, and inverter installation, all electrical connections and wiring, monitoring system integration, rigorous testing, and ongoing maintenance to ensure sustained optimal performance throughout the system's lifetime.

We pride ourselves on being a trusted partner in your transition to sustainable energy — handling the full scope of the installation so you can focus on your core business while we ensure your PV system is commissioned correctly, safely, and in full compliance with applicable standards and grid connection requirements.

Key Capabilities

  • Site assessment and system design
  • Solar panel and inverter installation
  • DC and AC electrical wiring
  • Grid connection and commissioning
  • Monitoring system integration
  • Rigorous testing and compliance
  • Ongoing maintenance support

What We Deliver

Full-Service Photovoltaic
Installation Solutions

Site Assessment

Detailed on-site evaluation covering solar irradiation analysis, structural assessment, shading analysis, and optimal panel placement to ensure maximum energy yield from your system.

Panel & Inverter Installation

Professional mounting and installation of solar panels, string inverters or central inverters, and all associated structural racking systems — optimized for long-term performance and durability.

DC & AC Wiring

Complete electrical wiring of DC solar circuits, combiner boxes, inverter connections, and AC grid integration with proper sizing, protection devices, and full labeling throughout.

Monitoring Systems

Integration of real-time energy monitoring platforms providing insight into system performance, energy production, fault alerts, and ROI tracking accessible from any connected device.

Testing & Commissioning

Comprehensive system testing including insulation resistance, IV curve tracing, protection device testing, and full commissioning to verify the system meets design and regulatory requirements.

Ongoing Maintenance

Planned maintenance services to sustain optimal system performance over the long term, including periodic inspections, cleaning support, performance analysis, and fault resolution.
