End-to-End Installation for Battery Production Facilities

INTERY D.O.O. offers comprehensive electrical and mechanical installation services for battery assembly lines, designed to meet the demanding needs of the rapidly growing energy and automotive sectors. Our skilled team ensures that every installation is carried out with precision, safety, and efficiency, supporting seamless operations in battery production facilities.

On the electrical side, our services include meticulous wiring and connections for power distribution and control systems, professional installation of cable management systems, full integration of control panels, and detailed labeling and testing for compliance with international safety standards. Every circuit is verified through comprehensive testing protocols before production commissioning.

On the mechanical side, we expertly install conveyor systems and support structures, set up assembly lines for various battery types, and ensure all safety features are correctly implemented. Our technicians bring extensive experience across electric vehicle battery manufacturing, renewable energy storage systems, and consumer electronics battery production — delivering end-to-end solutions you can rely on.

Key Capabilities

  • Power distribution and control wiring
  • Cable management system installation
  • Control panel integration and testing
  • Conveyor and support structure installation
  • Multi-battery-type assembly line setup
  • International safety standards compliance
  • Detailed labeling and documentation
